Designers I Admire

Aaron Walter

I first took notice of Aaron while he was working at MailChimp. I followed his UX blogging at MailChimp intensively and really learned a lot from the standards he put in place there. He now works for Invision and he's their go-to for education.

Rachel Nabors

Rachel is the author of Animation at Work which you can pick up from abookapart.com. I've been subscribed to her newsletter for ages and I really enjoy seeing the innovative animations she produces. Her book helped me get to the next level with my animation designs.

Gleb Kuznetsov

I started following Gleb about a year or so ago. I’m very inspired by futuristic designs and animations and this is something he specializes in. Everything I see him create completely fascinates me.

Steve Krugg

Steve preaches a common sense approach to usability almost everything he wrote in his book made me go "Oh yeah, that makes sense". His attitude to user testing really helped make ‘testing’ something to not be afraid of.

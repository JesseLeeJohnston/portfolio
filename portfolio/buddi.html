Project Summary

As a designer, I’ve always felt that people would look down on me if I had cannabis product in my portfolio. It’s been an industry I’ve always seen potential in but until recently it's always suffered from poor design. In April of 2017, I signed up to the local Vancouver Protohack event. I met Ryan at the event and learned about his project Buddi we decided to work together and actually wound up winning the event.

As of right now our product is being tested by a select few dispensaries in Vancouver and many different cannabis producers in British Columbia and Alberta.

Currently our MVP is a web based application, we’re looking at different options for making it a native application or a progressive web application. Since our dispensary partners are all primarily using iPads we have optimized it to look best in that context.

Dispensary customers put in a few details to start and eventually build out a profile. Based on their profile we connect them with products that we predict will be a good match for them.

Each products content is updated by the producer. As soon as they make a change that information is immediately updated on the dispensaries side. Producers are also able to get direct feedback from customers to help them make better decisions regarding their products.

Creating sketches and prototypes was critical to the success of the product. Since our team was for the most part working remotely I used InvisionApp to prototype designs prior to actually building anything. This also gave Ryan a chance to meet with dispensaries and get their feedback on the high fidelity mock ups.

As of right now we're testing the product with a few different dispensaries and getting their feedback.
